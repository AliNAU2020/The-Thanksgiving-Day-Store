This Thanksgiving story:
"1621: A New Look at Thanksgiving


by Catherine O’Neill Grace and Margaret M. Bruchac, illustrated by Cotton Coulson

Published by National Geographic, this unique book takes a different approach than most in attempting to revisit the first Thanksgiving from a Native American perspective


nstead of Disney-worthy illustrations, 1621 delights the eyes with photo essays, each painstakingly reconstructing the settlement where 52 English colonists sat down with 90 members of the Wampanoag tribe.


As visually arresting as it is educational, it’s the perfect read for a budding history buff.
